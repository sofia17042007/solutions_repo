Problem 1: Measuring Earth's Gravitational Acceleration with a Pendulum

The acceleration due to gravity, denoted g, is a key constant in physics. This experiment uses a simple pendulum to measure g and emphasizes: - Accurate measurement techniques - Uncertainty analysis - Comparison with the standard value:
$$ g_{\text{standard}} = 9.806 \, \text{m/s}^2 $$
